About MECHTech

What is MECHTech?

MECHTech is India's leading on-demand vehicle assistance platform that connects vehicle owners with verified mechanics for quick roadside assistance and scheduled services. We've transformed vehicle care by making quality service accessible, reliable, and transparent.

Whether you're stuck with a breakdown on the highway, need a flat tire fixed, or want to schedule a regular maintenance service, MECHTech gets you connected to trusted professionals within minutes.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To solve the problem of unreliable roadside assistance and make quality vehicle care accessible to everyone.

Vision

To be India's most trusted platform for vehicle assistance, delivering fast, reliable, and transparent support 24/7.
