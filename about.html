About MECHTech

MECHTech connects vehicle owners with verified mechanics for quick roadside assistance and scheduled services. Our mission is to deliver fast, reliable, and transparent support whenever and wherever you need it. We've transformed vehicle assistance by making quality service accessible to everyone in India.

Our Journey

Founded in 2026, MECHTech started with a simple vision: to solve the problem of unreliable roadside assistance. We recognized the frustration of vehicle owners stuck with breakdowns, uncertain about who to call and whether they can trust the mechanic. Today, we've built India's most trusted platform for on-road vehicle assistance.

How We Work

Smart Matching

Our AI algorithm matches you with the nearest verified mechanic based on your vehicle type, issue, and rating history.

Quality Assurance

Every mechanic undergoes rigorous background checks, skill verification, and customer rating monitoring.

Real-Time Tracking

Know exactly when your mechanic will arrive with live GPS tracking and instant notifications.

Transparent Feedback

Rate your experience instantly and help others find reliable mechanics in your area.
